What is Swing Trading?

Swing trading is a short-term trading strategy where traders aim to capture price "swings" over a few days to weeks. This strategy is ideal for those who cannot day trade due to time constraints or trading rules.

  1. Benefits of Swing Trading:
    • Lower time commitment than day trading
    • Potential for significant profits in a short period
    • Works well with technical analysis tools
  2. Risks to Consider:
    • Market volatility
    • Overnight price gaps
    • Requires discipline and technical knowledge

Common Swing Trading Strategies

Here are three widely used strategies for swing trading:

  • Breakout Trading: Entering a trade as the price breaks a key level.
  • Reversal Trading: Identifying a change in trend to buy low and sell high.
  • Momentum Trading: Riding a strong upward or downward price trend.
